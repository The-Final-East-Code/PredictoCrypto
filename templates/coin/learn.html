Cryptocurrency Cheatsheet

  1. Bitcoin (BTC)

    Introduced in 2009

    Considered the first and most well-known cryptocurrency. Operates on a decentralized network called blockchain.

  2. Ethereum (ETH)

    Introduced in 2015

    Decentralized platform for building smart contracts and DApps. Native cryptocurrency is Ether (ETH).

  3. Ripple (XRP)

    Introduced in 2012

    Digital payment protocol for fast and low-cost cross-border transactions. Used by banks and payment providers.

  4. Litecoin (LTC)

    Introduced in 2011

    Open-source digital currency created by Charlie Lee. Similar to Bitcoin but with faster transaction confirmation times.

  5. Cardano (ADA)

    Introduced in 2017

    Open-source blockchain platform for building decentralized applications and smart contracts. Designed for scalability and sustainability.
